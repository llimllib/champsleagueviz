League Winner Probability

Each team is placed on the chart by the median probability that they will win their league. Only teams with a >4% chance of winning their league are included.

This chart is automatically generated from the odds available at oddschecker, and will be periodically updated. If you want to see the code used to download the data and generate the chart, head over to github and check it out.
